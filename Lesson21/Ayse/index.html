Information About Zodiac Signs

Aries (March 21 - April 19)
Aries is a fire sign, known for leadership, courage, and initiative. Aries individuals are energetic, passionate, and ambitious.
Taurus (April 20 - May 20)
Taurus is an earth sign, characterized by patience, a need for security, and a practical mindset. They value material stability and comfort.
Gemini (May 21 - June 20)
Gemini is an air sign, known for their curiosity, sociability, and intelligence. They have strong communication skills and are known for their changeable moods.
Cancer (June 21 - July 22)
Cancer is a water sign, known for emotional depth, compassion, and protectiveness. They are very connected to family and home life.
Leo (July 23 - August 22)
Leo is a fire sign, known for confidence, creativity, and leadership. They have a strong charisma and are often the center of attention.
Virgo (August 23 - September 22)
Virgo is an earth sign, known for being analytical, organized, and detail-oriented. They tend to be perfectionists and are very practical.
Libra (September 23 - October 22)
Libra is an air sign, characterized by a quest for balance, justice, and harmony. They are social, polite, and seek peace in their relationships.
Scorpio (October 23 - November 21)
Scorpio is a water sign, known for passion, mystery, and charisma. They have a powerful inner world and are known for their emotional depth.
Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21)
Sagittarius is a fire sign, known for being free-spirited, adventurous, and optimistic. They love to travel and experience new things.
Capricorn (December 22 - January 19)
Capricorn is an earth sign, characterized by hard work, discipline, and goal-orientation. They are practical and responsible.
Aquarius (January 20 - February 18)
Aquarius is an air sign, known for their innovative, free-thinking, and independent nature. They are interested in social change and new ideas.
Pisces (February 19 - March 20)
Pisces is a water sign, known for empathy, emotional depth, and being dreamers. They have strong artistic and spiritual tendencies.
